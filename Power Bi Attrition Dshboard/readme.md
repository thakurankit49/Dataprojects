This project provides a comprehensive Predictive Risk Profile of employee attrition. By integrating organizational data, I identified key drivers behind turnover specifically targeting high-risk demographics and causal factors like overtime and compensation gaps.

The goal of this dashboard is to move beyond simple "reporting" and provide Actionable Intelligence for HR leadership to reduce turnover costs.
Through multi-dimensional analysis, I uncovered three primary "Danger Zones" for the organization:

1. The Overtime Trap: Employees working Overtime exhibit an attrition rate of 30.53%, which is 3x higher than those who do not (10.44%).
2. Entry-Level Vulnerability: There is a staggering 50.95% attrition rate among employees in the lowest income bracket ($0 - $5,000$).
3. Youth Retention Gap: The 18-24 age demographic shows the highest turnover at 39.18%, often correlated with low job satisfaction scores (Levels 1 & 2).
