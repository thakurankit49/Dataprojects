This Power BI dashboard provides a comprehensive 360-degree view of global automotive sales performance. By transforming raw transactional data into interactive visualizations, the report enables stakeholders to monitor key performance indicators (KPIs), identify seasonal trends, and analyze regional sales distribution to drive data-driven decision-making.

Key Business Questions Addressed:

1. Revenue Performance: The total revenue and how does the average deal price fluctuate.

2. Temporal Trends: Which months see the highest sales volume and where are the seasonal dips

3. Regional Analysis: Which cities and countries are the primary drivers of revenue

4. Product Portfolio: Which product lines (e.g., Classic Cars vs. Motorcycles) contribute most
